Resources & Suggested Screenings

Suggested screenings based on age and risk (general guidance):

  • Blood pressure — annually or as advised.
  • Fasting glucose / HbA1c — if overweight/obese or family history (start earlier).
  • Lipid profile — every 3-5 years or sooner if risk factors.
  • Liver function tests — if heavy alcohol use or metabolic risk.
  • Kidney function — with diabetes or hypertension risk.
  • Smoking cessation & lung screening — discuss with clinician if heavy smoker.
  • Mental health screening — when stress, sleep, or mood concerns exist.

These are general suggestions. Local guidelines and individual clinical context matter.
